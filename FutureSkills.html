Competition's I Can Do

Hosa Canada Competition

There are many different competitions in the HOSA Canada competition. They all revolve around medical healthcare, from nutrition to fitness. You can work in teams or individually. It is basically a test and a essay about everything you need to know about your topic. It showcases all the brightest students and can look very good for universities.

Youth Science Canada Competition

The Youth Science Canada competition is a fun competition and a science fair across Canada. You can choose several topics that relate to science. Some examples of winners from previous years include someone who made an AI that detects whether a tumor is malignant or benign. So there's definitely a lot of competition but it's a reality check for the near future.

Join or start a Medlife Chapter

The Medlife chapter program is an excellent way to show leadership and gather people to help in medical ways. You can showcase your capabilities and get recognition for it. Through the program, you can develop critical skills such as project management, teamwork, and effective communication. These experiences not only enhance your resume but also prepare you for future professional challenges.









Volunteering I Can Do

Canadian Blood Services

You can volunteer at most canadian blood services center. You must be 17-24 years old. These experiences can give you insight on your work enviorment for the future and can show you have clinical experience.

Peel Region Volunteering For Health

Peel provides many opportunities to volunteer in medical ways like volunteering in vaccination clinic which can showcase your leadership skills and oral communication.
